Reading naturally — quick tips

  • Talk, don't announce. Imagine explaining this to one person across a table, not addressing a room.
  • Slow down about 10% from your normal pace, and let punctuation breathe — pause at periods.
  • Mistakes are fine: pause, then re-say the whole sentence. Or just re-record — takes are cheap.
  • Room sound: soft furnishings beat empty rooms. Phone earbuds with a mic work surprisingly well.
  • Publishing: put downloaded files in the nxtphase/audio/ folder (keep the file name exactly as generated), then ask Claude to deploy. The app picks them up automatically.
